Instructions

You can rate the meaning relatedness of the pairs of words you will be shown on a scale from 1 to 5, where:

1 - completely unrelated meanings is for pairs of words that refer to completely different topics and carry completely different meanings;

5 - extremely similar meanings is for pairs of words that refer to two extremely similar topics and have extremely similar meanings.

Please rate I do not know one of the words if you are not familiar with the meaning of one of the words or both.

Never choose 'Do not choose this option'

Examples:

For example, 'aircraft' and 'plane' are higly related (5/5) because they are two words for the same object, 'aircraft' and 'car' are moderately related because they are two means of transport (3/5), 'aircraft' and 'hair' are not related at all (1/5).
