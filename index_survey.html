Instructions

You can rate the meaning similarity of the pairs of words you will be shown on a scale from 1 to 5, where:

1 - completely different meanings is for pairs of words that refer to completely different topics and carry completely different meanings;

5 - extremely similar meanings is for pairs of words that refer to two extremely similar topics and have extremely similar meanings.

Please rate I do not know one of the words if you are not familiar with the meaning of one of the words or both.

Never choose 'Do not choose this option'

Examples:

The words 'aircraft' and 'plane' have high similarity (5/5) because they are two words for the same object;

the pair 'aircraft' and 'car' are moderately similar (3/5) because they are two means of transport;

the words 'aircraft' and 'hair' are not similar at all (1/5).

We will check your adherence to the instructions above. If your answers are not satisfactory, we may exclude you from the study.

Other examples:

'Milk' and 'cow', 'hour' and 'time' have high meaning similarity (5/5)

'Horse' and 'cow', 'clock' and 'time' have moderate meaning similarity (3/5)

'Desk' and 'cow', 'grass' and 'time' have very low meaning similarity (1/5)
